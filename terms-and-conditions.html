Legal Framework

Terms & Conditions

These terms govern access to InternTheory digital services, APIs, and learning content. Usage implies acceptance of MSDE guidelines, applicable laws, and data-protection clauses.

Policy Version: 4.5 Effective: 01 Nov 2025

User Obligations

  • Provide accurate demographic, academic, and KYC information.
  • Do not share login credentials or misuse assessment material.
  • Adhere to classroom etiquette, anti-harassment norms, and IP laws.
  • Use forums responsibly; hate speech leads to immediate suspension.

Platform Commitments

  • Maintain 99% uptime for core learning services.
  • Provide audit trails of course completions and certifications.
  • Ensure accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA) for new features.
  • Support bilingual content and AI-based translation where feasible.

Liability & Dispute

  • Service is provided “as-is”; liability limited to fees actually paid.
  • All disputes governed by Indian law and courts in New Delhi.
  • Arbitration option through MSDE empanelled mediators.
  • Force majeure clause covers outages beyond reasonable control.

API & Integration Usage

Institutions using InternTheory APIs must adhere to throttling limits, data minimization, and secure storage requirements. Tokens are non-transferable and subject to review.

  • Notify us within 24 hours of suspected credential leakage.
  • Do not cache personal data beyond contractual purpose.
  • Allow penetration testing or compliance audits when requested.

Suspension & Termination

We may suspend services for policy violations, fraud, or non-payment. Users receive notice with a remediation window before termination.

Notice Period 15 Days

Standard time to address compliance deviations.

Data Retention 90 Days

Window to download transcripts post termination.

Legal queries or partnership contracts?

Write to legal@interntheory.com. Government departments can route files via MSDE e-office referencing “InternTheory Service Agreement”.

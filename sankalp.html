Skill Acquisition & Knowledge Awareness for Livelihood (SANKALP)

SANKALP modernizes the skilling ecosystem through decentralized planning, district skill committees, and disbursement-linked indicators that focus on quality, convergence, and market outcomes for every youth.

Loan No. 8741-IN MSDE + World Bank Closing: 2026

Focus Pillars

  • Institutional strengthening of state skill development missions.
  • Convergence across ministries via district skill committees.
  • Quality assurance through NCVET and national accreditation board.
  • Inclusion of women, PwD, and aspirational districts.

DLI Monitoring

  • Release of policy notes and improved data fidelity on national portals.
  • Operational district skill committees with annual skilling plans.
  • Better private sector participation via challenge fund projects.
  • Digital tracking of placements, apprenticeships, and entrepreneurship.

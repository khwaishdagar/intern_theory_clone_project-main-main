Inclusive Design

Accessibility Statement

InternTheory is committed to building barrier-free experiences that comply with WCAG 2.1 AA and Government of India Guidelines for Indian Government Websites (GIGW).

Accessibility Lead: Priya Kapoor Latest Audit: Sep 2025

Standards Followed

  • WCAG 2.1 AA for color contrast, navigation, and semantics.
  • Accessible Rich Internet Applications (ARIA) landmarks.
  • Keyboard and screen-reader compatibility across modules.
  • Captioning and transcripts for video/audio assets.

Assistive Features

  • Text scaling up to 200% without content loss.
  • High-contrast toggle and dyslexia-friendly fonts.
  • Voice-based navigation for selected flows.
  • Indian Sign Language micro-modules (beta).

Continuous Monitoring

  • Quarterly audits by NIC empanelled agency.
  • User testing with Persons with Disabilities (PwD).
  • Accessibility backlog tracked on the product roadmap.
  • Compliance status published in transparency portal.

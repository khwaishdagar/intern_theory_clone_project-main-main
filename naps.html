National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS)

NAPS incentivizes employers to hire apprentices by reimbursing stipend support and basic training costs, while providing youth with industry-recognized experience on the National Apprenticeship Portal (NAP).

DGFT + MSDE Portal 2.0 Updated: Nov 2025

Eligible Stakeholders

  • MSMEs, PSUs, and startups with UDYAM registration.
  • Institutes notified as Basic Training Providers (BTPs).
  • Skill Universities delivering on-the-job semesters.
  • Industry clusters running shared apprenticeship pools.

Financial Coverage

  • 25% of stipend (up to ₹1500 per apprentice per month).
  • Basic Training cost reimbursement up to ₹7500 for 500 hours.
  • Digital contract management & automatic PF/ESIC validations.
  • Unified credit flow with National Credit Framework (NCrF).

Compliance Automation

  • Auto-generation of apprenticeship contracts and NAPS IDs.
  • E-sign workflows with NSDC and State Apprenticeship Advisers.
  • Monthly muster roll, stipend traceability, and claim audit logs.
  • Real-time dashboards for establishments and sector councils.

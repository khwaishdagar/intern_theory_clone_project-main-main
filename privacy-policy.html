Privacy & Data Protection

We comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, IT Act 2000, and MSDE security advisories to protect every learner, recruiter, and institute.

Data We Collect

  • Identity: name, DoB, gender, photo, and government IDs (where mandated).
  • Contact: email, phone, address for communication.
  • Education & employment history for profile building.
  • Usage analytics (device, IP, session logs) for security.

Purpose & Lawful Basis

  • Service delivery, certifications, and placement matchmaking.
  • Regulatory reporting to MSDE, NSDC, or state skill missions.
  • Fraud prevention and platform diagnostics.
  • Consent-driven marketing (opt-in, revocable anytime).

Retention & Sharing

  • Records stored on NIC/MeitY compliant cloud within India.
  • Data shared only with verified partners bound by NDA/SLA.
  • Retention aligns with regulatory timelines (typically 8 years).
  • Data anonymized for analytics and published only in aggregate.

Your Rights

Users can invoke the following rights via dashboard or email:

  • Access and download personal data copies.
  • Correction or deletion (subject to legal requirements).
  • Consent withdrawal for marketing communication.
  • Grievance redressal within 7 working days.

Security Controls

How to Engage

Follow this protocol for faster approvals:

  • Email the SSDM CEO with a concise note and attach proposal.
  • Copy MSDE regional directorate and InternTheory partnerships team.
  • Request for slot in upcoming SSDM steering committee meeting.
  • Share quarterly progress decks aligned with district dashboards.

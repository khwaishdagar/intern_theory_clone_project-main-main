Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our refund framework follows Government of India directives for digital services, ensuring transparent timelines, auditable approvals, and learner-first escalation paths.

Policy Version: 2.3 Effective: 01 Nov 2025 Jurisdiction: New Delhi

Refund Eligibility

  • Full refund within 7 days of enrollment if sessions not commenced.
  • Pro-rata refund before 30% of sessions are consumed.
  • Assessment/certification fees are non-refundable once slot booked.
  • Scholarship-funded seats follow scheme-specific guidelines.

Cancellation Flow

  • Submit request from learner dashboard or email support@interntheory.com.
  • Receive ticket ID and SLA (3 working days) for acknowledgement.
  • Finance approval within 7 working days with bank transfer proof.
  • Disputes escalated to Grievance Officer and MSDE helpdesk.

Documentation

  • Invoice copy, payment reference, and learner ID mandatory.
  • Bank cancelled cheque or UPI ID for refunds.
  • In case of minors, guardian consent letter is required.
  • Audit-ready logs maintained for 8 financial years.

Processing Timelines

Refunds are credited back using the original mode of payment:

  • UPI/Wallets – 2 to 3 working days post approval.
  • Net banking/Cards – 5 to 7 working days as per issuing bank.
  • International cards – up to 12 working days with forex adjustments.

Refund status can be tracked live inside the learner dashboard with SMS/email alerts at every stage.

Alignment with MSDE Norms

We map our policy to MSDE circulars, Consumer Protection Act 2019, and RBI’s digital payment guidelines to prevent chargebacks or misinformation.

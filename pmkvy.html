Flagship Scheme

Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

PMKVY is India’s flagship outcome-based skill certification program that mobilizes youth, funds training partners, and aligns competency standards with industry councils so that every learner receives portable credentials and placement support.

MSDE + NSDC Version: 4.0 Updated: Nov 2025

Priority Tracks

  • Industry 4.0 job roles: EV maintenance, robotics, cloud infra, AI support.
  • Green economy skills for solar, waste-to-energy, and climate-resilient agriculture.
  • Service excellence in tourism, healthcare assistants, and fintech operations.
  • Regional languages, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship bootcamps.

Funding & Incentives

  • Performance-linked payouts for Training Partners and Assessment Bodies.
  • Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) stipends for informal sector workers.
  • Special projects for aspirational districts and North-East states.
  • Outcome verification through Aadhaar-enabled biometric attendance.

Digital Governance

  • Samarth portal for training center accreditation and geo-tagging.
  • Unified Skill India Digital ID for learners and trainers.
  • API integration with National Career Service and NAPS.
  • Live dashboards for ministries, state missions, and parliament queries.

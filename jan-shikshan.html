Jan Shikshan Sansthan & Skill Hubs

Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) transforms rural youth, women, and migrant workers through demand-led courses, functional literacy, and entrepreneurship support delivered in their local language with flexible timings.

MoE + MSDE Skill Hub Initiative Updated: Nov 2025

Priority Learners

  • Rural women self-help groups and micro entrepreneurs.
  • School dropouts transitioned via Samagra Shiksha.
  • Migrants and informal workers reskilling for services.
  • Artisans in ODOP clusters needing digital commerce support.

Learning Model

  • Short modules (30-300 hours) with bilingual facilitator guides.
  • Integration with PMFME, DAY-NRLM, and NSRLM fellowships.
  • Mobile vans and pop-up labs for seasonal outreach.
  • Micro-credentialing through Skill India Digital IDs.

InternTheory Toolkit

  • Community mobilization CRM with WhatsApp nudges.
  • Offline-first content player for no-network villages.
  • Marketplace plugin to showcase products from artisans.
  • Impact scorecards for CSR sponsors and district admin.

Skill Hub Alignment

National Skill Hubs connect ITIs/Polytechnics with nearby schools and JSS centers for after hours training. InternTheory schedules labs, faculty rosters, and credit transfer under NCrF.

  • Co-branded timetables and resource booking calendar.
  • Assessment rubrics mapped to NCVET standards.
  • Entrepreneurship mentorship with SIDBI/StartUp India.

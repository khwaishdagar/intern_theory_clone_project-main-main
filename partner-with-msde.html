Single Window Desk Response SLA: 5 Days

Who Can Partner

  • State Skill Development Missions & District Administrations.
  • Universities/colleges hosting skill hubs or finishing schools.
  • Industry bodies, MSMEs, and sector associations.
  • CSR foundations, multilateral agencies, and NGOs.

Engagement Models

  • Co-branded digital academies with credit-aligned content.
  • Apprenticeship pipelines and job fairs curated per sector.
  • Innovation pilots funded under SANKALP challenge grants.
  • Shared service centers for assessments & placement tracking.

What You Receive

  • Access to Skill India Digital APIs and reporting dashboards.
  • Dedicated program manager and success playbooks.
  • Communication kits for community outreach.
  • Monitoring & evaluation templates aligned to MSDE KPIs.

Application Checklist

Submit the following via email or the partnership form to kick-start due diligence:

  • Organization profile, registrations, and governance structure.
  • Impact thesis, sectors of interest, and target geography.
  • Existing infrastructure (labs, classrooms, hostels).
  • Compliance history (PF/ESIC, audits, CSR Form 1, etc.).

Due Diligence Timeline

Desk Review 5 Days

Review of documents, proposal, and initial viability.

Field Validation 10 Days

Virtual/on-site verification with state authorities.

Final MoU is issued after MSDE approval and alignment with relevant schemes (PMKVY, NAPS, SANKALP, etc.).
